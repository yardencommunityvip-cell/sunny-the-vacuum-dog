What's the story about?

Meet Sunny - a golden, playful pup with a talent for vacuuming up every crumb that falls under the table! He and his best friend Kai were born on the same day and have grown up side by side. But one afternoon at a birthday party, Sunny gobbles up something he shouldn't - chocolate and grapes! What happens next is a sweet, funny lesson that kids AND parents will love. 🎉
