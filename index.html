The Story

Born on the same day.
Best friends forever.

Sunny is golden and bright, with a tail that wags from morning to night. His best friend Kai drops every crumb and pancake and noodle - and Sunny? Sunny vacuums them all up with the greatest of ease.

"Oops! Goes a pancake. Plop! Goes some cheese.
Sunny vacuums them up with the greatest of ease.
Vroom, vroom! Goes the vacuum dog!"

But when a birthday treat lands on the floor - a dark brown square - Kai shouts "Stop!" just a moment too late. That night, Sunny's tail stops wagging. And that's when everything changes.

A warm, rhyming adventure about friendship, mischief, and the most important lesson a dog-loving family can learn together.

For Parents

More than a bedtime story - it's the safety lesson your family needs.

Every year, thousands of dogs are rushed to emergency vets after eating food that fell from the family table. Most families simply don't know which foods are dangerous - until it's too late.

Sunny the Vacuum Dog changes that. While your child giggles at Sunny's antics, they're quietly learning the most important pet safety rules - taught by a real veterinarian, through a story they'll want read over and over again.

By the time the book ends, your child won't just know the lesson. They'll be the one reminding you.
